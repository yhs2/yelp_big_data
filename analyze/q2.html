Relation between User/reviews/tips

The fig.1 shows the amount of reviews of each state. We found that the dataset are mainly from states Washington, Oregons, Colorado, Texas, Ohio, Georgia, Florida and Massachusett.

The fig.2 shows the distribution of all the 5 star business, and with darker the color of the map region, more of the 5 star business allocation.

Fig.3 is calculated on the number of tips and reviews written on the same business. Most people have an equal number of tips and reviews. However, some people prefer to give more tips than reviews on the same business, which reflect the potential improvement of each business. On the other hand, some people tend to have more reviews than tips, which means that they either they have a attitude turning on a specific business or they just simply adore or fascinating with their experience.

From the Fig.4 compliments count, we can clearly see that people who make both tips and reviews have a better overall reputation.
